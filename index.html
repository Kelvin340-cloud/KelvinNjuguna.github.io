About Me

Data Scientist | Machine Learning Enthusiast | Passionate About Impact

Hello, my name is Kelvin Njuguna Njoroge, and I am a data scientist with expertise in data analytics, machine learning, and AI. I thrive on transforming raw data into meaningful insights that drive impactful decision-making. With a strong foundation in Python, SQL, Power BI, and Excel, I specialize in techniques such as data visualization, exploratory data analysis, and predictive modeling to uncover patterns and trends.

Beyond data, I have a passion for research and sustainable development, and I enjoy working on projects that have a positive social impact. I also find joy in reading, playing chess, and swimming, as these activities fuel my curiosity and focus.
