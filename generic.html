Chocolate Company Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

Developed a dynamic Power BI dashboard to uncover key sales drivers for a fictional chocolate company. Analyzed $21M in profit from 6,000 shipments and 2M boxes, with December 2023 as the peak month ($3.0M). Identified the U.S. as the top market (19% of sales) and 50% Dark Bites as the leading product ($1.335M, 27% margin). Empowered strategic decisions through interactive visuals, MoM/YoY trends, and performance insights by product, region, and salesperson.
